# Code of Conduct

We want Opcore to be a useful place to work on agent-safe developer tooling.

Be direct, kind, and specific. Assume good intent, but do not excuse harmful behavior. Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or repeated disruption are not acceptable in issues, pull requests, discussions, or maintainer spaces.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation when conduct gets in the way of productive work.

To report a conduct concern, contact the maintainers through the security and maintainer contact path listed in `SECURITY.md` if the report should stay private.
